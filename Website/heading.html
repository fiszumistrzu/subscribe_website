What is the language diet?

Language diet it's a method of learning languages in different way. Stop repeating the same exercises, making next lesson on dualingo or rewriting sentences. Every diet should be balanced it's why I am going to serve you every week for one year different ideas how you can study differently. 55 weeks = 55 ideas what if one approach will be much better than every other methods to which you got used to learning at school?

How language diet
can improve your speed of learning?

Eating the same things regularly over and over make your body used to them. The same is with method of learning.

Good diet should be adjusted to person, if one method don't fit to you, no worries, all of us have kind of food which we ommit or we are alergic.

When you decide to get diet you have some goal. You need to decide do you want to get weight, loose weight or build abs for coming holidays. Finds areas where you want improve your grammar, learn new vocabulary or meaby practice your accent before planned date with foreigner?

Diet's should cointains different ingredients like protein, coal etc.
The same with learning we will try to get knowledge not only by one sense, watching games or sth else.

You need to enjoy. Eating things which you don't like quickly will discourage you from keeping diet. Try find methods which gave you a lot of fun and make learning languages the best from spending your free time.
